The Landscape

The Agentic Coding Landscape

The tools are evolving fast

Each tier trades control for automation. More autonomy = more power + more risk.
Level 0

Chat-based

Tools

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini

How It Works

Copy-paste. You are the executor.

Integration

None — fully manual

The simplest form. You ask a question, get code, paste it into your project. No integration with your codebase.
Level 1

Editor-integrated

Tools

Copilot, Cursor, Windsurf

How It Works

Inline suggestions. AI sees your files.

Your Role

You drive, AI navigates

AI has context of your open files. Tab-completion on steroids. You're still driving, but AI is navigating.
Level 2

CLI Agents

Tools

Claude Code, Codex CLI, Aider

How It Works

AI reads, writes, and executes. You supervise.

Your Role

Approve or reject changes

The agent can explore the codebase, make changes across files, run tests, and iterate. You approve or reject.
Level 3

Orchestrators

Tools

Gastown, OpenHands, Devon

How It Works

Multiple agents. Delegated work.

Your Role

You architect, agents execute

Multiple agents working in parallel on different tasks. You define the work, the orchestrator assigns and coordinates. Full autonomous "build me an app" is the frontier.
