Identified vulnerabilities should be fixed within a specific time, based on their severity as rated by the Common Vulnerability Scoring System (CVSS).

All critical vulnerabilities, with a CVSS rating between 9.0 and 10.0, are required to be remediated within 14 days of detection. Those classified as High, with CVSS scores ranging from 7.0 to 8.9, must be addressed within 30 days. Additionally, vulnerabilities assessed as Medium, having CVSS ratings between 4.0 and 6.9, are to be resolved within a 90-day period.
