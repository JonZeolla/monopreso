Automating the setup

  1. Automated Certificate Management Environment (similar to the never-officially-published SCEP) allows automated verification for certificate vetting.
  2. Cloud providers have APIs and automation, such as AWS Certificate Manager.
  1. Some more information on the wiki page

Keep in mind

  • It is critical to keep your private information secret
  • Avoid storing it on unsecured or third-party systems and ensure it is encrypted
  • Anyone with the private information can impersonate you on the Internet
  • In some cases they can also monitor your user's activities
  1. Even publicly-trusted CAs aren't impervious to attack. See this study.

Keep in mind

  • Private information should be unique to the instance of an application that's using it
  • Avoid sharing private keys and thus avoiding wildcard certificates
