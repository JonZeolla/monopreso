Developer Gotchas

  1. RSA is needlessly error-prone in implementation
  2. Many TLS libraries, OpenSSL included, are bloated and over-complicated
    RSA is error-prone
  1. While RSA is not currently under realistic threat by quantum computing (Shor's algorithm), RSA implementations are frequently implemented improperly. Trail of bits did a presentation and blog post in 2019 covering the details.
    TLS libraries
  1. OpenSSL is the swiss army knife, was audited in January 2019, and has a bug bounty program.
  2. Alternatives include BoringSSL from Google, BearSSL from Thomas Pornin, and LibreSSL which is an OpenSSL fork intent on modernizing the codebase and improving security.
