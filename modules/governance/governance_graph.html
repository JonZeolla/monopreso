Example InfoSec Policy

The scope of this policy extends to all employees, contractors, and third-party associates of FinTech Innovations, Inc. who access, process, or store company data. It applies to all forms of data, including electronic and physical formats, and encompasses all systems, networks, applications, and devices managed by the company.

This policy is integral to our overall risk management strategy and is mandatory for all personnel to adhere to, ensuring that every individual understands their role in safeguarding the company's information assets.
